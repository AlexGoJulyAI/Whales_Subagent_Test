Mockup C Right-Rail Status — Previous Conversations Redesign
What this changes

The Previous Conversations tab currently shows timestamps with no way to search or see outcomes without opening each conversation. This direction adds a search bar and gives every conversation an AI-generated title, then splits each card into two zones: a wide left area showing the title and a short preview snippet, and a narrow right column showing submission status and outcome — so the result of every attempt is visible at a glance without clicking into it.
