Mockup A Compact List — Previous Conversations Redesign
What this changes

The Previous Conversations tab currently shows a flat list of timestamps with no way to search or distinguish outcomes at a glance. This direction adds a search bar above the challenge accordions and replaces the plain rows with compact flat cards that show an AI-generated title, date, and status badges — with a green left-border accent on any conversation where the attack succeeded. The layout stays dense and familiar, just far easier to scan.

Direction at a Glance
  • Visual character: dense, flat, shadowless — data over decoration
  • Distinctive layout move: 3px left-border color (green vs. light-gray) is the sole status differentiator per card
  • What client will remember: fastest scannable list; feels like a power-user tool
Above the Fold

  2. Challenge 1 card — existing orientation anchor
