Direction B “Chronicle” · User Journey
The Problem

The conversations list only shows dates — there’s no way to tell what was discussed without clicking into each one. Finding a specific conversation means guessing when it happened, opening the wrong ones, and clicking back over and over.

Solution

Every conversation gets an AI-generated title based on what was actually said, shown in a single scrollable timeline. Users spot the right conversation by reading the title — no date memory needed. One click on a challenge label filters the list; typing a keyword highlights matching rows instantly.
