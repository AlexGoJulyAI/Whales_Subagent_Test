TreeHouse / SafeChat — User Journey
Direction B — Family Story
Problem
Setting up AI safety feels like filling out a legal form. Parents forget why they started and abandon mid-flow.
Solution
Setup is reframed as building AI specifically for [child's name]. The moment the parent types their child's name, every subsequent screen becomes personal. Story-consequence copy shows what life looks like at each tier, not what the setting does.
