Strategy
Direction B exists because the §3 problem for power users is not that filters are confusing — it is that they are absent. The persistent sidebar solves §12 Task 2 (filter by narrator name, genre specialty, voice style, language, most-listened) by presenting all five dimensions simultaneously, matching the mental model of advanced search that power users already use on other platforms.
The §4 secondary metric (filter panel self-explanatory) is trivially satisfied because every filter group has a label and is always visible.
