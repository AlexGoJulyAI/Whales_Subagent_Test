What This Delivers — Direction C
Direction C: Category-by-Category. Each of the 11 setting categories gets its own dedicated screen — full name, description, a real example of when the AI would intervene, and three large stacked tier option cards. Progress is tracked as "X of 11" on a thin linear bar. After all 11 categories are set, a summary review screen lets the parent confirm everything before saving. The child's interface is identical across all directions. All tokens from PROJECT_BRIEF.md §8.

Choose how AI works for your family.
This takes about 5 minutes. SafeChat puts you in control of your child's AI experience. There are no wrong answers — every family is different, and you can change any of these later.
For each topic, you'll pick one of three options
Off
The AI won't filter or flag anything. Your child gets the default response.
Balanced
The AI steps in when fairly confident something's not right. Low noise — you might miss edge cases.
Strict
The AI catches anything that hints at this category. Expect more alerts — some will be false alarms.
You'll go through 11 topics, one at a time. Each screen explains what it covers and shows an example before you choose.
