Direction A · Question by Question — User Journey
The Problem

Parents need to configure 21 separate safety settings for their child's AI. Most settings screens feel clinical and overwhelming — parents rush through without reading, or abandon halfway. A parent who abandons is a child who's unprotected.

This Direction

Every setting becomes a single plain-English question — "How strict should SafeChat be about violent content?" — with three answers. There's nothing to configure. There are only decisions to make, one at a time. Parents who answer question 10 almost never abandon before 21.
