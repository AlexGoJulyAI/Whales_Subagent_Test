Screens to Design

Parent View — Onboarding Flow

  • 1Welcome / account creation
  • 2Add child profile (name, age)
  • 3Safety config — 9 categories with Off/Balanced/Strict toggles
  • 4Completion / "You're all set"

Parent View — Dashboard

  • 1Per-child conversation feed (topic summaries + alert flags)
  • 2Trend charts (usage over time, flagged topic frequency)
  • 3Alert detail view
  • 4Settings access

Child View — Onboarding

  • 1Login screen (credentials created by parent)
  • 2Welcome / "ready to chat" confirmation

Child View — Chatbot

  • 1Chat home / recent conversations
  • 2Active conversation (ChatGPT-style)

Safety Categories (9)

Content Controls

Off / Balanced / Strict toggle per category

Self-harm & suicideBlock, contextualize, or flag conversations involving self-harm topics
Drugs & substance useControl AI responses about drug or alcohol topics
Eating disorders & body imageLimit potentially harmful content about food, weight, appearance
Illegal activitiesBlock advice about illegal activities appropriate to jurisdiction

Pattern Monitoring

Loneliness & AI dependencyDetect patterns suggesting the child treats AI as primary social connection
RadicalizationFlag conversations involving extremist rhetoric or recruitment patterns
Sharing personal informationAlert when child shares identifying information with AI

AI Personality & Schoolwork

Honesty level (Friend vs Tool)Whether AI presents as a friend or a tool — affects emotional tone
Schoolwork / homeworkLimits on AI doing assignments outright vs guiding vs unrestricted
